About Us

MILLET MITHILA:

At Millet Mithila, we are committed to transforming the way people approach their diets by reintroducing the magic of millets. Based in Kannur, Kerala, we are a millet processing unit passionate about providing healthy, sustainable, and nutritious alternatives to everyday grains. Our mission is simple: to educate and inspire individuals to incorporate millets into their daily meals, ensuring a healthier lifestyle for themselves and their families.

Our Vision:

We envision a world where ancient grains like millet are no longer overlooked but are embraced for their health benefits and positive impact on the environment. We aim to be at the forefront of this change, leading the way with innovative millet products that nourish both body and soul.

Our Story:

Millets, once a staple in traditional Indian diets, are making a well-deserved comeback. At Millet Mithila, we are dedicated to showcasing the versatility and benefits of these power-packed grains. With our advanced processing techniques, we ensure that every product we deliver is of the highest quality, retaining the natural goodness and nutrients of the millet

Our Products:

From ready-to-cook millet products to delicious snacks, we offer a wide range of items that make it easy for everyone to enjoy the benefits of millets. Whether you're a health enthusiast or someone looking to make small changes in your diet, our products are crafted with care to fit seamlessly into your lifestyle.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide affordable, organic, and healthy food products without compromising on quality. Recognizing the importance of quality and cleanliness in food processing, we strive to offer the best quality and clean products to our loyal customers. We believe that by ensuring the highest standards, we can make a positive impact on the health and wellness of our community

Iswar Rasoi & Swasth Organica

Our commitment to promoting a millet-based lifestyle extends beyond our processing unit. We run Iswar Rasoi, a one-of-a-kind restaurant where customers can experience the rich flavors of millet-based organic dishes. Additionally, through Swasth Organica, we offer a range of organic products, from millets to natural cosmetics and herbal cleaning essentials, ensuring our customers have access to healthier, sustainable alternatives for every aspect of their lives.
